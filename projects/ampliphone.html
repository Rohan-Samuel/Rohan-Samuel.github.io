Ampliphone

A wooden phone stand that boosts the sound coming from your phone speaker!

Overview

A small wooden device that acts as a phone stand. It also features two holes which can open and close using a lever to enhance the volume of your phone speakers.

Contributions

I worked on the technical drawing part of the planning process, making sure the sketches were to size so that they can be lazer cut precisely. I also created renders using different materials using Maya to showcase different options for the final device.

Images

A closed version of the lens-inspired speaker holes. You can control the aperture using the lever on the side.

Renders made using Maya to imagine the final product

The two pieces were designed to fit together as a box to be carried around. They also have magnets inside which lock them in place
